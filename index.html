Jenga is a Kenyan e-commerce platform. The foundation of the company comprises of a marketplace that contracts with building supplies, links manufacturers with consumers, and provides a platform for contractors to offer their services. Its logistics service enables the shipment and delivery of items from manufacturers to consumers, as well as convenient connections between constructors and consumers, and the company's payment service facilitates transactions among players active on its platform in designated markets.
